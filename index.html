E-Cell IARE: From here to eternity

  • E-Cell IARE founded November, 2021
    E-Cell IARE was formed with the aim of fostering entrepreneurial sprit within campus
  • Ideathon November, 2021
    Ideathon was conducted by E-CELL in association with TIIC. It aimed at evaluating the ideas of the students and also awarding the best idea.
  • Freshman Orientation December, 2021
    The first ever E-Cell Orientation was conducted on 2nd of December to explain the working of the Cell to interested students.
  • METE project expo December, 2021
    METE Project expo Was conducted by the CELL in collaboration with TIIC, to guide students in prototyping and evaluate the projects of the students across the various departments.
  • Logo Launch February, 2022
    E-Cell Launched it's logo
  • Discord server launch March, 2022
